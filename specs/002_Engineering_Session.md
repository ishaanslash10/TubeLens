# Specification 002

## Title

First Engineering Session

---

You are joining the TubeLens project.

Before beginning implementation, understand the repository.

Please read and use the following documents as your primary source of context:

- README.md
- AGENTS.md
- context/PRODUCT.md
- context/CONSTITUTION.md

After reviewing the project, do not generate any code.

Instead:

1. Explain your understanding of TubeLens.
2. Describe the product philosophy.
3. Identify the strengths of the current project foundation.
4. Point out anything you believe is missing before implementation begins.
5. Suggest improvements to the repository structure, documentation, or engineering workflow.

Treat this as your first engineering meeting with the project founder.

Do not begin implementation until alignment has been achieved.
