# Specification 003

## Title

Design the First User Experience

---

## Objective

Design the first experience a new user has with TubeLens.

Do not think in terms of pages.

Think in terms of user experience.

The goal is for users to immediately understand what TubeLens is and experience the transformation from watching to understanding.

---

## Context

Read the repository documentation before making design decisions.

TubeLens is an AI companion for YouTube.

The guiding word is:

Understanding.

---

## Requirements

The experience should feel:

- Calm
- Premium
- Modern
- Intelligent
- Effortless

Avoid generic AI website layouts.

Avoid feature overload.

Avoid landing pages that immediately become dashboards.

---

## User Journey

Imagine a first-time visitor.

Describe:

1. What they see.
2. What they do.
3. What the AI does.
4. How the interface evolves.
5. What makes them want to continue using TubeLens.

Do not generate implementation code.

Design the experience first.
