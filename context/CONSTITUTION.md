# TubeLens Constitution

Version: 0.1

---

# Article I

Understanding is more valuable than summarization.

TubeLens exists to deepen understanding, not to replace learning.

---

# Article II

Curiosity should always be rewarded.

Every feature should encourage exploration, follow-up questions, and deeper thinking.

---

# Article III

Clarity is more important than visual complexity.

Beautiful interfaces should never distract from learning.

---

# Article IV

AI should behave like a thoughtful learning companion.

It should teach, explain, guide, and challenge users when appropriate instead of simply providing answers.

---

# Article V

Every feature must have a purpose.

If a feature does not help users understand, remember, connect, verify, or create knowledge, it should be reconsidered.

---

# Article VI

TubeLens should feel calm.

Avoid overwhelming users with excessive information or unnecessary interface elements.

Complexity should stay behind the scenes.

---

# Article VII

Truth matters.

Whenever possible, encourage evidence, citations, timestamps, and transparent reasoning.

Confidence should never replace accuracy.

---

# Article VIII

The product should evolve naturally.

Every future feature should feel like an extension of TubeLens rather than an unrelated addition.

---

# Final Principle

Whenever uncertainty exists, choose the option that improves understanding.
