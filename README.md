# TubeLens

> **From Watching to Understanding.**

TubeLens is an AI-powered companion for YouTube that transforms passive video watching into an interactive learning experience.

Instead of simply generating summaries, TubeLens helps users understand, question, explore, connect, and retain knowledge from every video they watch.

---

## Vision

To become the only AI companion users need for YouTube.

TubeLens transforms every YouTube video into an intelligent workspace where learning continues long after the video ends.

---

## Why TubeLens?

Watching a video is only the beginning.

Current AI tools often stop at summarization.

TubeLens goes further by helping users:

- Understand concepts deeply
- Ask questions naturally
- Visualize ideas
- Build long-term knowledge
- Verify claims
- Translate content
- Create notes and study material
- Learn with an AI companion

---

## Core Philosophy

TubeLens is built around one guiding word:

> **Understanding**

Every feature should help users move from passive consumption to active learning.

## Development Philosophy

TubeLens is more than an application. It is an exploration of AI Product Engineering, Prompt Engineering, and Context Engineering.

Rather than relying on one large prompt, the project uses structured context, product documentation, and iterative prompting to guide AI-assisted development.
